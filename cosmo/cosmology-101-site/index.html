Cosmology 101

The universe is 13.8 billion years old, 93 billion light-years wide, and mostly made of stuff we can't see. The more precisely we measure it, the stranger it gets. This is the whole story — the settled science and the beautiful unsolved paradoxes — across eight deep-dive chapters.
